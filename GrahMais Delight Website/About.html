About GrahMais Delight

GrahMais Delight is a small dessert business that focuses on serving creamy graham corn jelly dessert. The product is meant to be sweet, refreshing and accessible to various types of customers.

The business aims to provide a simple but satisfying dessert that can be enjoyed by students, families, workers, and dessert lovers. GrahMais Delight aims to provide a product that can be enjoyed, with high-quality ingredients and proper preparation.
